🍫+🍰Chocolate Cake Recipe

A rich, moist, and delicious chocolate cake you can make in under 1 hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Grease your baking pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix all dry ingredients together.
  3. Add wet ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.
  5. Let it cool before serving.
